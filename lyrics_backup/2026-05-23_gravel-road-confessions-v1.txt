[RAW LYRICS]
[Hook]
[Acoustic guitar intro - warm, intimate strumming]
I've been driving down this gravel road just to find your door
There's a fire in the midnight air that wasn't there before
Got the windows down but I'm burning up from the thought of you
Let me pull you in and show you what this country heart can do

[Verse 1]
[Soft rhythm kicks in - laid back pocket groove]
Your porch light's glowing like a beacon in the dark
Every step I take is syncing with the beating of my heart
You're leaning on the frame with that dangerous little smile
I've been waiting for this moment, honey, for a long, long while

[Pre-Chorus]
[Bass line swells, acoustic guitars shimmer]
We don't need no neon lights to set the mood right here
Just the crickets and the shadows fading as I pull you near
I can feel your breath against my neck, a sweet and sudden rush
Every secret that we're holding is about to break the hush

[Chorus]
[Full country-pop arrangement - warm and soaring]
So let's get lost out where the gravel road ends
Where the moonlight whispers and the riverbed bends
I want to trace every line of your beautiful frame
Until the morning sun rises and you're calling my name

[Post-Chorus]
[Echoing electric guitar, gentle drum groove]
Oh, you're calling my name
(Calling my name)

[Verse 2]
[Stripped back, intimate acoustic fingerpicking]
I see the reflection of the stars in your eyes
Tangled up in the sweet seduction of your midnight sighs
My hands are wrapped around your waist, pulling you in tight
We're leaving all our worries on the front porch tonight

(Calling my name)

[Bridge]
[Atmospheric acoustic guitar, dropping the beat for a reflective pause]
Lost in the echoes of the shadows and the light
There is a fire burning through the silence of the night
Destiny is pulling us closer than before
Every single heartbeat leaves us wanting more
Hold on to the feeling, let the rhythm take control
Say my name softly, Saeed in your soul
[Whispered]
(Saeed... Saeed...)

[Spoken]
[Music drops out completely, just close-mic vocals]
